His backstory

Elvis was born in June of 2015, but we didn't adopt him until national dog day in August of 2015. By that time he was almost 2 months old and he was the smallest little boy. It was love at first sight when we saw him. He was a scrawny little puppy when we adopted him, but he is now going to be 5 years old next June and he is as chubby and loveable as ever. I got very lucky. Some of his quirks is that he

  • Has a security blanket
  • He walks sideways when he get's excited
  • He kneads his blanket like a cat does
  • He always has to be covered up when he goes to bed

He may be a very spoiled boy he get's toys way too much, and eats way better then other dogs, but he's really a great, great dog.
